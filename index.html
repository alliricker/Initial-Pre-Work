Christine Kerry Ratcliffe

MA, LPCC, ATR-P

Christine recently graduated from Naropa University with a Master's Degree in Mental Health Counseling & Transpersonal Art Therapy. She is currently employed as a Child & Family Therapist at Mount Saint Vincent in Denver, Colorado.

Her experiences as a mentor, intern, & student have allowed Christine to share the healing qualities of art with children & adults alike. Presently, Christine is focused on working with children ages 5 to 12 who have experienced trauma & neglect. She is also a teaching assistant at Naropa University for the graduate level Child & Adolescent Art Therapy class.

When she's not working or teaching, Christine enjoys painting her own soothing scenes. She is also an avid world traveler, drawing inspiration from new lands & cultures. While at home, she loves to explore all that colorful Colorado has to offer.
